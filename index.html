About Elizabeth

Experienced. Knowledgeable.
Professional.

A Miami native, having personally lived in communities across the entire city ranging from Miami Beach to Doral, she personally understands the nuances of the various neighborhoods like very few people do. Her personal experiences as a community resident, homeowner and real estate investor make her uniquely qualified to assist buyers in their search for their next (or first) property. She specializes in relocations and enjoys assisting clients from the beginning of their search until they are settled into their new home. Her marketing strategies are anything but ordinary, ensuring that properties listed with Elizabeth will stand out.

Elizabeth's strength as an agent comes as no surprise given her professional background. She began her career on the Management Team for the largest infrastructure project in the United States at that time — The Miami Intermodal Center (MIC). While working as the Prime Consultant on projects throughout the city, her love of the unique towns and neighborhoods around Miami grew. Elizabeth's professional work includes the creation of Economic Impact Analysis Reports for Miami-Dade County, meaning she has first-hand knowledge of city plans and forecasts.

Elizabeth personifies a rare breed of residential real estate consultant whose forward thinking and unmatched local knowledge deliver the clear-cut results her clients demand. She provides a custom-tailored approach from consultation to closing. Her ability to tenaciously negotiate on her client's behalf is matched by having their best interests in mind at every stage of the transaction.

Elizabeth's energy and enthusiasm coupled with her strong business acuity guarantee client satisfaction. Her reach and reputation have garnered great relationships with a curated network that spans luxury, tech, innovation, sports and celebrity. Her loyalty and discretion ensure complete professionalism and confidentiality at every touch point.

Among her distinguished clientele, Elizabeth has had the privilege of representing professional athletes, entertainers, and accomplished executives — each transaction handled with the utmost care, confidentiality, and white-glove service they deserve.
